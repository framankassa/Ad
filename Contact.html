Our Story of Ethiopian Elegance

Where modern luxury meets timeless Ethiopian hospitality

The Sunshine Legacy

Founded in 2010, Sunshine Hotel was born from a vision to create Addis Ababa's most exquisite hospitality experience, blending Ethiopian heritage with world-class luxury.

Our Founder

Alemayehu Kebede, visionary entrepreneur

Sunshine Hotel began as a dream of our founder, Alemayehu Kebede, who envisioned a hospitality experience that would showcase Ethiopia's rich cultural heritage while providing unparalleled luxury. After years of meticulous planning and construction, we opened our doors in the heart of Addis Ababa's prestigious Bole district.

Today, we stand as a testament to Ethiopian excellence, having hosted dignitaries, celebrities, and discerning travelers from around the world. Our commitment to authentic Ethiopian hospitality has earned us numerous awards and recognition as one of Africa's finest hotels.

"We don't just host guests, we welcome them into our Ethiopian family."

- Alemayehu Kebede, Founder
