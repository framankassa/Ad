Ethiopian Coffee Ceremony

Experience the authentic Ethiopian coffee ceremony, a centuries-old tradition that transforms coffee preparation into an art form. Our skilled barista will roast green coffee beans right before your eyes, filling the air with aromatic flavors.

  • Traditional roasting over charcoal
  • Three rounds of serving (Abol, Tona, Baraka)
  • Accompanied by popcorn and incense
